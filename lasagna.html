Lasagna Recipie

In this recipie we will learn how to make lasagna. this is one of the highest rated lasagna recipies on All Recipes

Ingredients you will need

  • Meat of Choice choose beef chicken or turkey (i prefer beef)
  • Onion Dice 'em up
  • Tomatoe Sauce This Sauce is the Boss
  • Herbs consisting of, but not limited to parsley and garlic
  • Sugar just a dash

  1. Cook The Meat
  2. Cook The Noodles
  3. Make the Cheese Layer
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
